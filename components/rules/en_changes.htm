Update: April 5, 2019

  • Registered teams will first face-off in the Playoff Stage of the Regional Qualifiers. The exact format of this tournament is subject to change depending on the number of teams registered.

Update: March 15, 2019

  • Base Prize Pool increased to $2,000 USD.
  • Major Regional Qualifier tournament removed, Minor Regional Qualifier now named "Regional Qualifiers" and will be the only Regional Qualifier all teams play in to qualify for The Feast World Cup: Main Event.
  • FRC invitations removed. All registered teams must start from the beginning of the tournament and register through the registration form to confirm participation.
  • Regional Qualifier - Group Stage will only occur as a preliminary if more than 8 teams are registered (per region).
  • Europe PvP Teams vs Japan PvP Teams matches will all be played on the Aether Datacenter. Read FAQ for more information.
  • As a result of the previous change, Europe PvP Teams and Japan PvP Teams that qualify for The Feast World Cup: Main Event will only be required to make 1 additional PvP-eligible character on the Aether Datacenter.
  • The Feast World Cup: Main Event Group Stage format adjusted (from 2 groups of 6 teams to 4 groups of 3 teams). Read FAQ for more information.
  • Tournament dates slightly adjusted following tournament format changes.
